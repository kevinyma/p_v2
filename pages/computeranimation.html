Cornell Computer Animation

Team: Manuela Rios, Rachel Freeberg, Kevin Ma, Adam Bergere


I spearheaded the visual design and information architecture of the animation club's new website.

Research

The project began with collecting the client’s and target audience’s needs and wants for the website. Personas were crafted from this initial investigation.

Process

The old site is dated and it shows: too much text and far too little images, and the information architecture is confusing. The website does not instill confidence that the Cornell Computer Animation Club is a creative leader.


I thought about design solutions that addressed the client's needs. Regarding the visual design of the site, I began to think how the new site could put visual content at the forefront.


Based on the needs of our personas, I mapped out a new information architecture.



Design

I wanted to design the website as to present the club’s work in a way that entices students around campus to join, and also that gives pride to the current members of the club.
